What I Do

From creating engaging newsletters, promotional materials for events, and graphic work for web and print, I design striking materials backed by data. If there's a method to improving designs for clients (whether that takes the form of A/B testing, digging into customer demographics, or analyzing website trends) I'm going to do whatever it takes to turn those findings into measurable results.

Who I Am

Oh hi! This is me.
After studying math at the University of North Texas to become an actuary, I came to realize that accounting is a bit of a bore and I like living life on the creative side. So, I took a chance with a marketing engineering job at a small gym and never looked back. I try to find ways to use my love of math, data, and creativity in nearly everything I do. Learning new skills, keeping up with the latest design trends, and constantly being outside of my comfort zone is where I thrive. Send me a message if you like!
